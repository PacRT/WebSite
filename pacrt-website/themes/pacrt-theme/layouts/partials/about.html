What is PacRT DewDrop?


PacRT DewDrop is a Secure IoT Appliance that can be used where low latency, high throughput and real-time is required in connected devices business use cases. DewDrop IoT Appliance can be used wherever you have business needs to collect data to send to your server infrastructure/enterprise applications and services. DewDrop is a real-time, secure and scalable platform that provides a simple connection to the cloud at scale. Advanced security features, application and integration services and support, with deployment from cloud to edge.



Security

  • PacRT DewDrop becomes the secure gateway from your connected IoT devices into your Enterprise. You can focus on developing your business applications and servcies around your products or market.
